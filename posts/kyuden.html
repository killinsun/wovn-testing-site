About "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service"

  • Q.​ ​What kind of service is "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service
    A.

    "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service" is a monthly delivery of meter reading slips (notification of electricity usage, notification of gas usage, notification of Purchased power amount), and the Internet from a smartphone or PC. It is a service that you can check the usage details on the dedicated site of.

    By registering as a member, we will notify you by e-mail, LINE, short message, etc. that the monthly details have been confirmed.

  • Q.​ ​Is "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service" available for all rate plans?
    A.

    It can be used with all rate plans (electricity contract, purchase contract, gas contract) except for high-voltage contracts and flat-rate contracts.

    ​Click here the rate plans available for the Web statement service

  • Q.​ ​Who can use “Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service”？ 
    A.

    This site can only be used by the contractor, those who belong to the corporation or organization, or the family members who have given consent from the contractor.

  • Q.​ ​What kind of data can you see?
    A.

    You can see information such as monthly billing amount, usage amount and fund transfer result. You can also refer to past usage details. Specifically, the following contents can be viewed.

    ■ Electricity / purchase / gas contract information

    ■ Electricity / gas usage / billing amount

    ■ Electricity / gas fund transfer results

    ■ Purchased power amount / payment amount

  • Q.​ ​I have subscribed to "Kyuden Gas", can I use the Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service
    A.

    Customers who subscribe to "Kyuden Gas" can also use the "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service".

  • Q.​ ​Is it possible to check the sales record of solar power generation on the "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service
    A.

    Customers who have a low-voltage purchase contract (power Kyushu Electric Power) such as solar power generation can also check the power sales record on Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service

    * You cannot use Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service Kyushu Electric Power Electric Power Transmission and Distribution Co., Ltd.

  • Q.​ ​Is it possible to check past usage, billing amount, and payment amount?
    A.

    * Since it is intended for use after May 2020, the maximum display will be less than 24 months until April 2022.

    * The history before March 2021 (before the start of this service) is a simple display. Please note.

  • Q.​ ​Is it possible to output a receipt?
    A.

  • Q.​ ​I am using the "Web version of the meter reading slip" of KIREI-LIFE-PLUS. Can I also use the Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service
    A.

    * You cannot log in to the "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service KIREI-LIFE-PLUS Sorry to trouble you, but please register as a member separately.

  • Q.​ ​If I register as a member of the "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service", will a paper meter reading slip be issued?
    A.

    If you register as a member of the "Kyuden Web Statement Service", we will stop issuing paper meter reading slips.

  • Q.​ ​Can I use it on mobile phones other than smartphones?
    A.

    For mobile phones other than smartphones (feature phones: flip phone), it may not be displayed correctly.

  • Q.​ ​Is it free to use?
    A.

    You can use "Kyushu Electric Online Statement Service" free of charge.

    * However, it is necessary for the customer to prepare the communication equipment such as smartphones and the Internet connection environment required to use this service. In addition, a separate communication fee will be charged for browsing the site, which will be borne by the customer.

  • Q.​ ​Is there a fixed time zone that can be used?
    A.

    In principle, it can be used 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

    * However, the service may be temporarily suspended due to system maintenance, etc.

About usage and display contents

  • Q.​ ​When will the usage statement (purchase statement) be updated?
    A.

    As a general rule, the usage statement (purchase statement) will be updated within 2 business days from the Meter reading date

    You can check the date of your next meter reading in the "Next meter reading date" column of the "Statement" screen after logging in.     

    * Updates may be delayed due to delays in meter reading data received from Kyushu Electric Power Transmission and Distribution Co., Inc.

  • Q. My​ ​electricity contract has been renewed, but my gas contract has not. Is the timing different?

    Okay. Even in the same household, the electricity and gas Meter reading date are different, so the update date of the usage statement is also different.

  • Q.​ ​What is "estimated usage"?
    A.

    If the meter cannot be read due to an emergency disaster, etc., the charge will be calculated based on the usage amount (= estimated usage amount) of the previous month.

    From the next month onward, when the meter is read correctly, the charge will be calculated again based on the correct usage amount, and the difference will be settled.

  • Q.​ ​What is the "minimum monthly fee"?
    A.

    This is the lower limit of the electricity bill you pay in a month. If the sum of the basic charge and the Power usage charge is less than the minimum monthly charge, the total of the minimum monthly charge and the Renewable energy surcharge will be charged.

  • Q.​ ​What is "Renewable energy surcharge
    A.

    All customers who use electricity are responsible for promoting the spread of renewable energy.

    The cost of purchasing renewable energy (solar, wind, small and medium-sized hydropower, geothermal power, biomass) is a part of the electricity bill, "renewable Renewable energy surcharge", and is available to all customers according to the amount of electricity used. The burden is stipulated by the "Act on Special Measures Concerning the Procurement of Renewable Energy Electricity by Electricity Companies".

  • Q.​ ​What is the "Adjustments for fuel costs, etc.
    A.

    Based on the fuel cost adjustment system, if the fuel price of crude oil, liquefied natural gas, or coal rises or falls below the standard average fuel price, the electricity price is adjusted accordingly.

  • Q.​ ​How is the amount of "use until ____________" calculated?
    A.

    This is the current electricity rate (provisional rate) based on the amount of usage up to XX (the day before login) during the target meter reading period. This is only a reference value and may differ from the actual billed amount. Please note that if the amount of usage cannot be obtained normally due to communication problems, etc., only the range that can be obtained can be displayed.

    (Method of calculation)

    Electricity rate = basic rate (daily rate) + Power usage charge ± Adjustments for fuel costs, etc. + Renewable energy surcharge

    • Basic charge: Monthly basic charge is calculated by prorating the number of days that have passed up to the previous day during the meter reading period.

    Power usage charge: Calculated by multiplying the amount used up to the previous day by the Power usage charge (applicable unit price is prorated)

    Adjustments for fuel costs, etc.: Calculated by multiplying the amount used up to the previous day by Fuel, etc. adjustment unit price for the target month

    Renewable energy surcharge: Calculated by multiplying the amount used up to the previous day by the unit price of Renewable energy surcharge for the target month

    *Electricity rate discounts (Bank transfer discount, 2-year contract discount, Sukusuku Baby Plan, etc.) and supplementary contracts (Avispa Fukuoka Support Plan, Whole Renewable Energy Plan, etc.) are based on estimated electricity rates. etc. are not included in the price for reference.

    *In the first month of the contract (before the first billing), Adjustments for fuel costs, etc. are not included, and the meter reading period is calculated as 30 days.

    * The unit price of each charge applies the unit price at the time of display.

  • Q.​ ​How is the "estimated electricity rate" calculated?
    A.

    Estimated electricity charges for the target meter reading period based on usage up to XX (the day before login). Based on the most recent usage, we estimate the amount of usage for the remaining days of the meter reading period and calculate the fee.

    (Method of calculation)

    Electricity rate = basic rate + Power usage charge ± Adjustments for fuel costs, etc. + Renewable energy surcharge

    • Basic charge: Monthly basic charge

    Power usage charge: Calculated by multiplying the expected usage by the unit Power usage charge

    Adjustments for fuel costs, etc.: Calculated by multiplying the estimated usage amount by Fuel, etc. adjustment unit price for the target month

    Renewable energy surcharge: Calculated by multiplying the expected usage amount by the unit price Renewable energy surcharge for the target month

    *Electricity rate discounts (Bank transfer discount, 2-year contract discount, Sukusuku Baby Plan, etc.) and supplementary contracts (Avispa Fukuoka Support Plan, Whole Renewable Energy Plan, etc.) are based on estimated electricity rates. etc. are not included in the price for reference.

    *In the first month of the contract (before the first billing), the estimated charges are not calculated and displayed as "-".

    * The unit price of each charge applies the unit price at the time of display.
